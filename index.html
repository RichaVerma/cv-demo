Richa Verma

Resident Enterpreneur, SocialCops

Richa Verma works as a Resident Entrepreneur at SocialCops and is passionate about solving problems at the intersection of data and technology. A graduate from LSR, she has extensively worked in deploying decision systems for governance, impact management, and development planning using data. At SocialCops, she has been involved in varying capacities across functions and projects, including our work on the DISHA Dashboard and in helping Government of Maharashtra create rapid development plans for villages like Chandrapur using our data intelligence platform. From taking various leadership roles during college to today leading the Research and Analytics arm first, and now the People Ops function at SocialCops, Richa has built her expertise in building, strategizing and leading solutions for large complex problems using data and technology. She has also represented SocialCops as a speaker, a delegate, or as an attendee at various national and international conferences.

Education

  • Lady Shri Ram College for Women, Delhi University
    At LSR, Richa served as the President, Economics Department. Prior to that she worked as a Coordinator for Vountary Agency Placement Programme.
